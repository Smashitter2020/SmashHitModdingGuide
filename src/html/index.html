Welcome to Smash Hit Modding Guide!

Smash Hit is a game developed by Mediocre AB, is a forward-scrolling mobile game where players throw metal balls at glass obstacles. Its elegant design, procedural feel, and satisfying physics have made it a beloved classic since its 2014 release. Modding Smash Hit means modifying the game's internal files — XML level data, Lua scripts, textures, meshes, and audio — to create entirely new levels, obstacles, visual themes, and gameplay mechanics.
